Stive Jobs

Stayed 15 Apr, 2020

Booked a 2 bedroom suite using my timeshare points through RCI back in April for a July 2-9 stay. The only glitch was I called Hilton directly to confirm my reservation and they couldn't find it despite the reservation number provided by RCI. I contacted RCI and they assured me I had a reservation. When I arrived my name was registered and check-in was a breeze. Our suite was a huge 2 bedroom with a full kitchen, dining area and living room. Having stayed at other timeshare properties, I knew what they layout would be but my brother and his wife were totally blown away. It was worth it just to see their faces. The resort was quite busy. It's clearly a family oriented place and the pool was the place to be. The weather was well over 100 degrees the whole week so we mostly soaked in the pool and went out for dinner. We chose to make breakfast every day in the room, which is a huge money saver. The staff was very friendly. I'm sure they were all happy to be back at work. They didn't have an airport shuttle but that is apparently in the works. Things at this resort (as well as other hotels/casinos we went to) are still adjusting to the post-pandemic crowds. Fortunately, this meant we weren't asked to sit through a timeshare presentation. That area seemed locked up and they are probably getting staff ready to start doing that again. They do have a marketplace on the first floor where you can get all your last minute needs (frozen meals, drinks, coffee, etc.). They serve Starbucks in there as well. The pool stayed open until 10 pm, which was a nice bonus. The nights were still in the 90's. Overall a wonderful room and stay. I would gladly book another stay here! One more thing - I didn't get my receipt by email as promised, so I called the Hilton number and again they couldn't find my reservation! It's as if by booking through RCI it only existed there on site. They had to finally transfer me to the front desk of the actual resort to look up my reservation and resend the receipt (they had the email wrong). Strange. There seems to be a missing link between RCI and Hilton, but it didn't affect my stay. Still had a wonderful time.
